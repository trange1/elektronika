Finding and Replacing Characters

To find characters:

  1. Choose Find from the Edit menu to bring up the Find dialog box.

  2. Enter the characters to find.

  3. To find the characters in a text region, make sure "Find in text regions" is selected. To search through math regions, select "Find in math regions." To find characters in collapsed areas, select "Find in collapsed areas." You may search through all of these types of regions and areas simultaneously.

  4. Click "Find Next" to continue.

To find and replace characters:

  1. Choose Replace from the Edit menu to bring up the Replace dialog box.

  2. Enter the characters you want to find next to "Find what."

  3. Enter the replacement characters next to "Replace with."

  4. You may then:

  • Click "Find Next" to find and select the next instance of the item.

  • Click "Replace" to replace the currently selected instance of the item.

  • Click "Replace All" to replace all instances of the item.

Characters You Can Find and Replace

You can use the Find dialog box to locate a variety of numbers, letters, and other characters in a worksheet. Numbers and letters can be found in both text and math regions. However, some characters can only be found in certain types of regions, such as strings. Use the table below to see which characters you can search for in specific regions.
