Custom Characters Toolbar

Some characters that can be used for variable and function names are difficult to type in Mathcad. This toolbar lets you enter them in math regions by clicking the buttons. Choose View > Toolbars > Custom Characters to display the toolbar. The currently available characters are:

°F, °C, /°F, /°C, ±, ≈, Ο, and ||

The temperature functions and inverse temperature functions are predefined in Mathcad to assign and convert values with temperature units. The other symbols are not predefined, they are available for you to assign your own functions and variable names.
