Filtering with a Gaussian kernel

gaussconv(M, s) Convolution of matrix M with frequency domain gaussian of half-width s.

gaussdeconv(M, s, e) Deconvolution of matrix M with frequency domain gaussian of half-width s with error e.

Arguments:

  • M is an image matrix in the spatial or frequency domain.
  • s is a nonnegative scalar used to determine the Gaussian half-width; it must be less than 700.
  • e is a positive scalar.
