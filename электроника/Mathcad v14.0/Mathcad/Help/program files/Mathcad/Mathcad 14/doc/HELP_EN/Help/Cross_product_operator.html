Vector Cross Product

Keystroke: [Ctrl] 8

u × v

Returns the vector cross product of u and v.

Operands:

  • u and v are three-element column vectors.

Notes:

  • Direction of the cross-product is orthogonal to u and v in the direction determined by the right hand rule.

  • Magnitude of the cross product is |u|·|v|sin(θ) where θ is the angle between u and v.
