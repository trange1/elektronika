You can choose to show or hide other toolbars from the View menu.

Math toolbar

Each button in the Math toolbar opens another toolbar of operators or symbols.

Notes:

  • To learn what a button on any toolbar does, let the mouse hover on the button until a tooltip appears with a brief description.
  • To detach and drag a toolbar around your window, place your cursor anywhere other than on a button or a text box. Then hold down the mouse button and drag.
  • You can customize the Standard, Formatting, and Math toolbars. To add and remove buttons, right-click on the toolbar and choose Customize from the menu.

When you exit and re-open Mathcad, the elements are positioned as they were when you exited.
