Wavelet packet bases

create_level(n,d) Returns a d-dimensional wavelet packet basis of all wavelet packets at the level n.

wavebs(n,d) Returns the d-dimensional wavelet packet basis corresponding to the wavelet transform of level n.

Arguments:

  • n is a non-negative integer.
  • d is 1 or 2, representing the dimension of the data being processed.
