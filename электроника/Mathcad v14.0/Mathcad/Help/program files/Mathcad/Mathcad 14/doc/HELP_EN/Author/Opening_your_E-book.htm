Opening Your E-book

To open your E-book:

  1. Start Mathcad and choose E-Books > Open Book from the Help menu.
  2. Browse to the location of your HBK file and click on it.
  3. Click "Open."

Your book opens in the E-book window. Use the toolbar buttons at the top of the window to browse the book. For example, click to go to the Table of Contents, or click to open the searchable index. Readers of your E-book can annotate it through the book menu.
