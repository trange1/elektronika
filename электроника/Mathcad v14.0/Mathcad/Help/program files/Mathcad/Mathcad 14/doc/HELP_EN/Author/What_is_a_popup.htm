What Is a Pop-up?

A pop-up is a small window that appears in the Mathcad viewing area when you click a pop-up hyperlink. Pop-ups are usually very short pages displaying extra information about a topic without taking up the whole window.

Pop-up files are useful for specifying extra details or presenting solutions without taking the reader away from your main flow of information. You create a pop-up file as a full Mathcad file, just the hyperlink to it is designated as a pop-up.

Tips:

  • To create a pop-up file, make a narrow Mathcad file generally about one screen or less to avoid forcing the reader to scroll through it.
  • Store pop-up files in a subdirectory called Pop-ups, for easy organization.
