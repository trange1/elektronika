Page Setup Dialog Box

The Page Setup dialog box contains the following:

Size

The size of the paper in your printer.

Source

The area in your printer supplying the paper.

Portrait

Positions the paper vertically.

Landscape

Positions the paper horizontally. Use this setting if you are printing a worksheet that is wider than a single page.

Margins

Left

The distance from the left edge of the paper to the left edge of the print area.

Right

The distance from the right edge of the paper to the right edge of the print area. This appears as a solid vertical line in your window. You may have to scroll to the right to see it.

Top

The distance from the top edge of the paper to the top edge of the print area. If your worksheet has a header, it appears just above this margin.

Bottom

The distance from the bottom edge of the paper to the bottom edge of the print area. If your worksheet has a footer, it appears just below this margin.

Print single page width

Prints only regions to the left of the right margin.

Printer

Provides access to your current printer settings.
