Copying Information from an E-book

To copy an entire section from an E-book to your worksheet:

  1. Choose Select All from the Edit menu.

  2. Click on the Copy button on the E-book toolbar.

  3. Click in the main worksheet window.

  4. Choose Paste from the Edit menu.

To drag a region from an E-book to your worksheet:

  1. Select the regions you want to move.

  2. Move the mouse pointer into one of the regions enclosed by a dashed selection rectangle. The pointer turns into a small hand.

  3. Hold down the mouse button and drag the regions from the E-book to the worksheet.

  4. When the regions are in the desired position, release the mouse button.
