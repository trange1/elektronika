Reading Messages in the User Forums

When you first open the User Forums, you will see how many messages are new and how many are addressed to your attention. You can view "All Messages" or only those that are "New" since your last visit. To read messages in any forum:

  1. Click on the forum name.

  2. Click on a message to read it, or on the "+" to view the replies underneath it.

Messages that you have not yet read are shown in italics. You may also see a "new" icon next to the new messages.

New Flags

To see New flags next to messages that have been posted since your last visit, choose More in the User Forums menu bar, then open Edit Your Profile. Under "Automatically mark new msgs read" choose "No."

After reading new messages, you can choose Mark Read from the User Forums menu bar, and "Mark All Forums Read" or just select individual forums. The next time you visit, only new postings will have the New flag.
