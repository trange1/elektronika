Checking your Web site

To check your finished Web site:

  1. Save all Mathcad worksheets to be included in the site as HTML files. Make sure that the filenames match hyperlinks and scripting used in your worksheets and templates.
  2. Open the first page containing your table of contents. This file is probably named index.htm or default.htm. Click on every link to check your hyperlinking structure. All links should be correctly translated to .htm files, and all content from your templates inserted around the HTML from your Mathcad worksheets.
  3. Correct any filenames, template issues, and alignment problems with Mathcad regions that did not export as expected.

Once your Web site is complete, you can copy your .htm files and their associated image folders to any internal or external Web server.
