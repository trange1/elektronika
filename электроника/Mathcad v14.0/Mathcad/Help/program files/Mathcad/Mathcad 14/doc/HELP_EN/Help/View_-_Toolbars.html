To Move a Toolbar

  1. Place the cursor anywhere other than on a button or a text box.

  2. Press and hold down the mouse button and drag.

Some toolbars rearrange themselves depending on where you drag them while others retain their shapes.

To Learn What a Button on a Toolbar Does

Hover over the button with your cursor in the Mathcad window until you see a tooltip description and a message in the status bar below.
