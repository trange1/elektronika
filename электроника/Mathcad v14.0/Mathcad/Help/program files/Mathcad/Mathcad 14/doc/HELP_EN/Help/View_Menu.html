View Menu Commands

Toolbars

Hides or displays toolbars.

Ruler

Hides or displays the worksheet and text ruler.

Status Bar

Hides or displays the message status bar at the bottom of your main worksheet window.

Trace Window

Hides or displays the Trace Window, used for debugging programs, at the bottom of your main worksheet window. The Trace Window can be moved and redocked by pressing and holding the [Ctrl] key and moving the window with the mouse.

Header and Footer

Defines design elements in headers and footers.

Regions

Displays all regions in the default background color leaving the rest of your worksheet gray.

Annotations

Displays colored parentheses around all annotated regions in your worksheet.

Refresh

Re-displays the worksheet.

Zoom

Zooms the view in or out when you choose the percentage of magnification.
